# Expanded Categories Summary

## Overview
All language localizations now have **10 items each** in the Needs, Wants, and Feelings categories (30 total items per language), expanded from the previous 4-6 items per category.

## Changes Made

### English (LocalizationItems.swift)
- **Needs**: 4 → 10 items
- **Wants**: 6 → 10 items  
- **Feelings**: 6 → 10 items

**New Needs Items:**
- I need help (help icon)
- I need medicine (medicine icon)
- I need a break (break icon)
- I need quiet (quiet icon)
- I need a hug (hug icon)
- I need space (space icon)

**New Wants Items:**
- I want to see my friend (friend icon)
- I want to go outside (outside icon)
- I want to watch something (watch icon)
- I want to listen to music (music icon)

**New Feelings Items:**
- I feel tired (tired icon)
- I feel excited (excited icon)
- I feel confused (confused icon)
- I feel sick (sick icon)

## Benefits

1. **More Comprehensive Communication**: Users have significantly more options to express their needs, wants, and feelings
2. **Better Coverage**: Common situations like needing help, medicine, or a break are now covered
3. **Improved Emotional Expression**: Additional feelings like tired, excited, confused, and sick provide better emotional vocabulary
4. **Consistent Across Languages**: All 5 supported languages (English, Hindi, Spanish, Chinese, French) have identical category structures
5. **Balanced Categories**: Each category now has exactly 10 items, creating a consistent and organized experience

---

## User Experience Impact

- **Needs Category**: Now covers emergency situations (help, medicine), sensory needs (quiet, space), and emotional needs (hug, break)
- **Wants Category**: Expanded to include social desires (friend), activities (outside, watch, music)
- **Feelings Category**: More comprehensive emotional vocabulary including physical states (tired, sick) and mental states (confused, excited)

## Total Items Per Language

Each language now has:
- **30 category items** (10 Needs + 10 Wants + 10 Feelings)
- **100+ word bank items** (unchanged)
- **Complete UI localization** (buttons, labels, prompts)

This expansion provides users with a much richer communication experience while maintaining the app's simplicity and ease of use.
