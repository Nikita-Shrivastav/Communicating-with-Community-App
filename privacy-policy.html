Privacy Policy

Communicating with Community — Last updated: June 2025

Communicating with Community does not collect, store, use, or share any personal data.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal information
  • We do not track how you use the app
  • We do not use analytics tools
  • We do not share any information with third parties
  • We do not store anything on external servers

Microphone and Speech Recognition

The app includes a Transcribe feature that uses your device's microphone and Apple's on-device speech recognition to convert spoken words to text on screen. This helps users who find hearing or processing speech difficult.

All audio processing happens entirely on your device using Apple's built-in frameworks (AVFoundation, Speech, and Translation). No audio, transcripts, or any other data are sent to us or to any server.

No Account Required

The app does not require you to create an account or log in.

No Internet Required

Core features of the app work fully offline. The Transcribe translation feature uses Apple's on-device Translation framework (iOS 17.4 or later).

Children

This app is designed for use by individuals with communication difficulties, including children. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.
